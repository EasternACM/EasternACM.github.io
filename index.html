    FAQ

    How do I join Eastern ACM?

    To join you will need to sign in your eaglesync using your EMU ID and then join the ACM group by clicking here or using the search bar. You will be able to see our next meeting time and place under the events tab.

    What is a Hackathon?

    A hackathon is a 24-36 hour event on weekends, where college students come from near and far to collaborate on hardware related and programming projects. The food and whole event is always free. Also, you are often given a free t-shirt and a collection of goodies that vary at each hackathon. Students at all levels are welcome to attend, so it can be very easy to learn from or help teach your peers. The students work together to design their very own ideas and usually end up presenting their work in front of judges for a chance at cool tech-related prizes. Hackathons are typically sponsored by companies in the software business, so it is also a great place to connect with potential employers.

    How do I sign up for the next hackathon?

    Many of the hackathons that ACM members attend can be located on Major League Hack's website. For a list of some upcoming hackthons in North America click here.
