About Us

With a unique model of operation, we are more than a typical collegiate club.

Focused on accelerated learning, we hold bi-weekly workshops centered around sharpening of our skills.

A collaborative and supportive environment, native forum inclusive, we revel in each other's successes and take pride in our open-source and community driven work.

Proud to be Eagles, we are the University of Eastern Michigan ACM.

Membership

Favoring a task-based model of operation, each member will be required to contribute towards the entity's upkeep by performing tasks beneficial to the organization.




Tasks will include

Seeking sponsorships.

Coordination of events the organization may attend, such as hackathons that are fully sponsored for students.

Providing mentorship and support for newer members.

Searching for ways in which the organization may participate in community enriching, or open-source work.
